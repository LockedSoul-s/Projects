# Solo Projects

Independent notebooks built during self-improvement practice to explore time-series forecasting and hierarchical modeling.

## Contents
- **Hierarchical_energy_forecasting.ipynb** – hierarchical time series forecasting experiment with multi-level energy data.
- **walmart-weekly-sales-forecast.ipynb** – weekly sales forecasting with time-series feature engineering and evaluation.

## Skills highlighted
- Time series preprocessing and feature engineering
- Forecasting model evaluation and comparison
- Working with hierarchical or multi-level datasets
